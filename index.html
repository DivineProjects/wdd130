Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country of southern Africa. It shares a border with South Africa, Botswana, Zambia, Namibia and Mozambique. Zimbabwe is completely within the tropics but enjoys subtropical conditions because of its high average elevation.Guarantee fine weather there during the cool, dry winter months from May to August, with average temperatures between 13° F (7° C) and 91° F (33° C). Make sure to visit the one of the 7 wonders of the world, Victorial Falls, and the biggest collection of wildlife,incuding, the rhino, wildbeast, elephant, lion.
