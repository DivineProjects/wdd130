Overview

Purpose

To create an online presence that reaches out to all adventurers who seeks guided, safe and rough waters.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The targeted audiance are all water rafting sports persons seeking a place to train, individuals and families looking to try out water rafting as a recreation and tourists looking for adventure. Who are the target customers? Water Rafting sports persons, individuals and families, and tourists. Describe their interests? Should be interested in training, learning to raft or looking for a recreational activity. What do they want? They want an affordable and thrilling white water rafting environment, with high safety standards and guides. How will my audience usually access my site? They will use a desktop or laptop device.
